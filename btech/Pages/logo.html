Bay-Tech

Bay-Tech is unique in its ability to tackle projects engineering challenges on land, shore and at sea. Expertise in naval architecture and marine engineering is our heritage. BayTech has earned its reputation across industry for highly engineered and cost effective design with timely delivery. And continue to serve naval architecture services to clients like Bashundora Group (One of the largest business conglomerate in Bangladesh,Seven Ring Cements, BIWTA (Bangladesh Inland Water Transport Authority) and many others. But beyond our skill in naval architecture, we offer a broad range of expertise that includes Structural, Mechanical, Electrical engineering; Engineering Simulation & Analysis; marine and facilities construction management; transportation planning and marine research.
